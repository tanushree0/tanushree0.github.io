About

I did undergraduate studies in Information Technology from DA-IICT Gandhinagar, and masters in Information Design from UC Berkeley. I've over six years of work experience in web development and design with companies like Microsoft and startups in Bay Area and India.

I am also a trained Expressive Arts Therapist. I have a committed interest in the space of self exploration and expression, using different arts and mediums. I'd be happy to work together if we share common interests.
