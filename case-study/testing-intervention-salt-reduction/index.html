Testing intervention for reducing salt in home-cooked food

How might we enable behaviour change of consuming home-cooked food with 50% less salt? This is the long-term goal that the team was designing for, through a design sprint.

Study objective

Evaluate the efficacy of messages delivered to rural households for reducing salt in home-cooked food

Methods
  • Concept testing
  • Field study
  • Interviewing
Outcome

Insight on current habits and attitudes, and which messages are working well. Refined strategy for moving forward.
